Our Story

We at Shear Genius believe that going to the barbershop should be more than an errand but an experience to remember. Our mission is to ensure that every customer who walks out of our chair has not only received a new haircut, but impeccable service, and the confidence of a new person. Above all else, Shear-Genius is a professional barbering and hair-styling establishment that prides itself on customer satisfaction, and commitment to serving the needs of our community.
